Installation made simple.
When the vehicle software update arrives on your center display, install or defer it.
When parked in a safe location and connected (with either a data plan or Wi-Fi®), follow the on-screen prompts to install.
After installation, which can take up to 20 minutes, swipe left and tap the Alexa app to begin setup.
